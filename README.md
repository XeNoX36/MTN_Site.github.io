# MTN_Site.github.io
This app analyzes MTN customer churn in the first quarter of 2025 in Nigeria

# Business Implications and Recommendations  
**Targeted Retention Campaigns**:  
Implement predictive churn modeling to proactively engage high-risk customers, especially younger demographics and users with low satisfaction scores.  

**Improve Service Quality in Key States**:  
Invest in network upgrades in high-churn regions identified by the dashboard to reduce customer turnover.  

**Enhance Loyalty Programs**:  
Offer personalized discounts or data bonuses for long-tenure and high-value customers to improve retention.  

**Monitor Subscription Plan Performance**:  
Regularly review underperforming plans and adjust pricing or value propositions to remain competitive.  

**Customer Feedback Loop**:  
Integrate sentiment tracking from satisfaction scores to inform customer service improvements and maintain brand loyalty.
